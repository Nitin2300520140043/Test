# üè° House Market Analysis Dashboard Power BI

## üìä Project Overview
This Power BI project provides a comprehensive overview of the **housing market**, focusing on sales performance, pricing trends, and regional insights.  
The dashboard helps users explore key market indicators such as **median price changes**, **sales growth**, **price-to-offer analysis**, and **regional performance**, offering a data-driven perspective for real estate decision-making.

---

## üéØ Objectives
- Analyze **house sales trends** across regions and sales types.  
- Compare **offer price vs. purchase price** to assess buyer-seller dynamics.  
- Evaluate **average sales and price per square meter (SQM)** by region and house type.  
- Identify factors influencing **purchase prices** using Power BI's **Key Influencer** visual.  
- Provide interactive filters for **area, city, sales type, and region** to support dynamic analysis.

---

## üß© Dashboard Pages Overview

### **1. House Market Overview**
This page provides a macro-level summary of housing market performance.
- **Median Sales Price Change by Region**- Bar chart comparing price variations across Jutland, Fyn & Islands, Zealand, and Bornholm.  
- **Units Sold (Year & Quarter)**- Displays total units sold.  
- **Last 12-Month Sales**- Shows total transaction value in billions.  
- **Purchase Price vs. Offer Price**- Scatter plot visualizing price correlation.  
- **YOY Sales Growth by Sales Type**- Line graph showing growth/decline in sales types like auction, regular sale, other sale, and family sale.

---

### **2. Sales Performance**
This page dives deeper into sales KPIs and influencing factors.
- **Sales by Region** -  Highlights total sales volume by region.  
- **Key Influencers Visual** -  Identifies variables (like house age) that affect purchase price.  
- **Offer to SQM Ratio by Sales Type**- Compares price efficiency across sales categories.  
- **Average Price SQM by Region**- Donut chart showing price per SQM and regional share.  
- **Sales Table**- Provides transactional breakdown by year, quarter, and day for trend tracking.

---

### **3. Price & Yield Analysis**
Focuses on pricing behavior by house type and financial metrics.
- **Filters**- Interactive slicers for Area, City, Sales Type, and Region.  
- **Avg Offer vs. Purchase Price by House Type**- Bar chart comparing pricing gaps across property types.  
- **Avg Inflation/Interest/Yield by House Type**- Displays financial indicators impacting pricing.  
- **Avg SQM/SQM Price by House Type**- Combines bar and line chart for detailed SQM pricing analysis.

---

## üí° Key Insights
- **Zealand** recorded the **highest total sales (95bn)**, while **Bornholm** had the lowest.  
- **Family sales** show the **largest YoY decline**, indicating potential buyer hesitancy in this segment.  
- **Purchase prices** increase significantly when the **house age exceeds 113 years** (as found via Key Influencers).  
- **Apartments and townhouses** show balanced performance in both yield and price per SQM.  
- **Minimal gap between offer and purchase prices** across most house types suggests a stable market.
